About Us

Mark and Luisa first met online on the cusp of March 2020. Little did they know, while the rest of the world would soon shut down, their hearts remained open. A nervous first date at Marks gym, led to late night conversations and being glued to their cellphones. During the shut downs, they safely went on dates hiking, walking, and developed a deep connection.

Mark, being an intelligent, caring, funny, and charming gentlemen found love with Luisa who is kind, sweet, sensitive,and goofy. Luisa shares a similar sense of humor as Mark, laughing at all of his corny jokes. They enjoy trying new resturaunts near and far.

Mark and Luisa are excited to be getting married, celebrating their love with friends and family. They look forward to building their life and love together.
